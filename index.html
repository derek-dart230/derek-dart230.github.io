The weight slowly shifting, falling towards the stern

It all slows to a stop, the water rushes by

A snap towards the end, a break thoroughly earned

In time they move from bow, a motion fully learned

A bark far in the stern, snaps them out of the trance

Masters of the craft, unfazed by the call
